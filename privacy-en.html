2. Purpose of Processing

We process personal data for the purpose of:

  • Connecting customers with contractors for service jobs
  • Communication related to a specific job (confirmations, notifications, scheduling)
  • Processing payments for services rendered
  • Complying with legal obligations (e.g. accounting and tax regulations)
  • Improving platform quality and security

3. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contract performance — processing necessary to facilitate and complete a job
  • Consent — for WhatsApp Business API communication, where explicit opt-in is required
  • Legitimate interest — fraud prevention, platform security
  • Legal obligation — accounting and tax record-keeping

4. WhatsApp Communication

MAJSTR uses the WhatsApp Business API (operated by Meta) to send job-related messages — e.g. job confirmations, registration links, new job alerts for contractors.

  • Messages are sent only to users who have given consent (e.g. by replying to an initial message or registering on the platform)
  • Message content is end-to-end encrypted (Signal protocol), consistent with standard WhatsApp infrastructure
  • Message metadata (delivery time, read status) is processed by Meta as the technical infrastructure provider
  • Users can opt out of communication at any time by replying "STOP" or contacting us via email

5. Who We Share Data With

Personal data may be shared with the following third parties, solely for the purpose of operating the service:

  • Meta / WhatsApp Business Platform — for message delivery
  • Stripe — for payment processing (Stripe Connect)
  • Twilio — for SMS/WhatsApp technical infrastructure
  • Supabase — for database storage and authentication

These companies process data under Data Processing Agreements (DPA) and in compliance with GDPR. We never sell data to third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Data Retention

  • Job and communication data: for the duration of the contractual relationship + 3 years (for dispute resolution purposes)
  • Accounting and tax records: per legal retention periods applicable in the Czech Republic (typically 10 years)
  • Marketing/communication data: until consent is withdrawn

7. Your Rights

As a data subject, under GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Object to processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing
  • Lodge a complaint with the Czech Office for Personal Data Protection (ÚOOÚ), www.uoou.cz

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at sir.kuba@seznam.cz. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse, including encrypted communication and restricted database access.
